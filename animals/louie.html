Dog

Louie

Also known as לואי

Meet our Louie — a tiny, gorgeous little dog in a wheelchair. He was attacked by pit bulls and suffered injuries to his hind legs, but with his wheelchair he gets everywhere. His previous owners could no longer care for him, so he came to the farm and joined our community of disabled dogs. He gets along wonderfully with the other dogs in the enclosure, and is always thrilled to meet new people and old friends alike.
