The Story of Yossi

Meet our Yossi. He was born on a dairy farm — where there's no use for male kids, and their fate is usually a bitter one within the industry.

A kind grandfather bought him as a Passover gift for his grandchildren, but once the holiday ended they had no idea what to do with the kid, or who would look after him. An animal isn't a toy you can set aside or throw away when you get bored.

A kid goat only a few days old needs care and bottle feeding every three hours, so the grandfather turned to the farm and asked us to take the little one in and care for him. Of course, we couldn't refuse — and that's how Yossi joined the family.
