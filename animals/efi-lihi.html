Calves

Efi & Lihi

Also known as אפי וליהי

The sweet calves from the Gaza-border communities have arrived — and we've already fallen head over heels for them. Beloved ones, welcome to your new home. After the hell you've endured, we promise you will be safe and protected here — we will care for you for the rest of your lives. Here you will live free, happy lives, full of love. They're already running and playing all over the farm, hopping around joyfully, grazing on grass with delight, and slowly meeting all their new friends. We chose to name them after two of our devoted volunteers, whom we love so dearly.
