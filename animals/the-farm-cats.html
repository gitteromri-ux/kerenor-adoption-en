Cats

The Farm Cats

Also known as חתולי החווה

One day the news reported that officials from the Ministry of Agriculture and the Tzfat municipality had removed many cats from a resident's apartment. The cats were in a horrifying state — sick, wounded and severely neglected. The Ministry of Agriculture described it as an extreme case known to the welfare authorities. The apartment in Tzfat was overwhelmed by a terrible smell. The cats lived in filth and suffered from skin fungus. The apartment's owner had once sold cats but over time lost control of the growing population — the animals were not vaccinated, spayed or neutered.
