Goat

Tami

Also known as תמי

Meet our Tami. Tami belonged to egg smugglers — they used to smuggle goats through the West Bank into Israel. Two young boys would walk her around, cracking a whip and beating her. Eventually, security guards from Oranit saw them hitting her, took her away from them, and kept her safe until we could come to bring her to us.
