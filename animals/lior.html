Cow

Lior

Also known as ליאור

Meet Lior, who came to us from the Be'er Sheva area. Volunteers found her in a sewage stream under a bridge in freezing Be'er Sheva — a young cow, limping and hobbling, her hind leg appearing to be broken. She was trembling and frightened, drained of energy and motivation, and wouldn't let anyone come near. It turned out she belonged to local Bedouins — she had gone out with them for a morning walk, slipped, injured herself and was left behind, and the herd's owner had no intention of coming back for her. Fortunately, we received permission to bring her to safety. After a 17-hour rescue operation, she finally made it to the farm.
