Dog (female)

Penny

Also known as פני

Meet our small, beloved Penny — a tiny puppy who just wants warmth and love. Penny was found by kind people while she was dragging herself from place to place using only her two front legs. It turned out that some children had beaten her so many times, and so brutally, that they broke her spine. Not yet four months old, and already her whole world had collapsed — she will be disabled for life. Our dear Tal Talmon sent us her story, and through her we met Shira, who rescued Penny and became a warm, loving, incredible foster mom for her.
