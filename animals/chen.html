Kid goat (female)

Chen

Also known as חן

In early May 2023 we received a call from a woman who said a paralyzed kid goat had been found in a field. She told us she was providing food and cleaning her, and asked us to take her in. After we approved, she brought her to us. The next day someone called and said he had heard we'd brought in a disabled kid goat and was inquiring about her — which was odd, because we hadn't posted anything about her. As the call went on, his tone grew more and more suspicious. Once the call ended, Keren Or called the woman who had brought her, trying to understand what was going on. The woman apologized for not telling the truth — she had thought that otherwise we wouldn't accept the kid.
