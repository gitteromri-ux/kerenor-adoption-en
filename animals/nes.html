Calf

Nes

Also known as נס

Three months ago, a true miracle happened — a tiny, extraordinary calf was born 47 days premature, in critical condition and with no chance of survival. But Nes (whose name means 'miracle'), a determined little fighter, managed to astonish us all. Today he's three months old, healthy, and full of joy and love for people. After everything he's been through, Nes still needed one more miracle — to be saved from a bitter fate and given a future in freedom, here at Keren Or Farm. So he can grow up alongside others of his kind and other animals, in a home full of warmth and love, and receive all the devoted care he deserves.
