Cow

Momo

Also known as מומו

Meet Momo, our cow. She was found running frightened along a road when she was only a day or two old. A couple traveling in the area saw her and were afraid she would be hit by a car. They managed to catch her. Feeling sorry for her — she had probably belonged to a herd somewhere nearby — they decided to save her. They brought her to Beit Netanel, where she was nursed and raised, and later they brought her to us at the farm.
