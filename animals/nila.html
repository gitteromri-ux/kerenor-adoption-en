Dog (female)

Nila

Also known as נילה

Nila (female) was diagnosed with a congenital defect at two months old that prevented her from moving her hind legs. Her owners, without a second thought, threw her into the street. Neighbors fell in love with her and decided to do everything they could to rehabilitate her. They gave her a warm home, showered her with endless love and care, and took her through every possible treatment and test with the best vets in the country. After complex surgery — intended to reduce her pain and enable her to get around with a wheelchair — she began a difficult rehabilitation period. In the end, Nila found her way to us.
