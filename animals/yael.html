Sheep

Yael

Also known as יעל

Meet our Yael. She was born in a dairy pen alongside many brothers and sisters. After a few days she began to limp, and no one knew what had happened — nor was it profitable to invest in tests or treatment. Yael arrived at Keren Or Farm with open wounds and a non-functioning hind leg. After being seen by five different vets, the decision was made to amputate the leg so she could live a full, healthy life. Today Yael is growing and recovering. From a young lamb, she is slowly becoming a strong, healthy ewe.
