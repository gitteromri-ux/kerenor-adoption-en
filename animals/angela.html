Angela

Also known as אנג'לה

Meet our Angela. She was found in Be'er Sheva after being abused — wounded, with graffiti drawn on her body. She arrived at the farm thanks to kind people who watched over her and brought her to us on the eve of Shavuot. From the moment she arrived, she has simply followed the volunteers everywhere, always seeking human affection.
