Donkey

Tzvika

Also known as צביקה

Tzvika arrived with Malka — a pair of innocent baby donkeys who had already endured neglect and deprivation in their short lives. They were rescued with great difficulty from their abusive owner. When they arrived at the farm, we discovered that Malka's condition was complicated and severe. She received special food and all the love we could give her, around the clock, but after several months of fighting for her life, our Malka collapsed and passed away. Tzvika, her brother — who had watched over his little sister constantly, circling her to make sure she was okay — was left alone. Today, he is part of a large, loving family here at the farm.
