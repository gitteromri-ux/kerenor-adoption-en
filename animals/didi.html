Kid goat (female)

Didi

Also known as דידי

Meet Didi — a playful, curious kid goat full of life, born on Valentine's Eve. Since then she's been racing around the farm alongside her mother Tamari, melting every heart she meets. A sharp-eyed volunteer noticed that her mother was showing signs of distress and called our incredible medical team, who did sacred work in helping the little one arrive safely. Since then, her mother has been caring for her devotedly, and we're doing everything we can to give her a good start with lots of love and care. But to make sure she has everything she needs, we need your help. Every donation — no matter the size — makes a real difference.
