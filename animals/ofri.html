Ofri

Also known as עפרי

Meet our small, beloved Ofri — a tiny calf born prematurely on a dairy farm. She came to the farm for close, ongoing care and bottle feeding every few hours. After several days she began eating hay on her own and bonded with Dolly the sheep — and the two have been inseparable ever since. Welcome to the family, little one.
