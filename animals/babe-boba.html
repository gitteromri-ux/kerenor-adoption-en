Pig

Babe & Boba

Also known as בייב ובובה

Babe, our sweet pig, came from the meat industry. He was bought for a petting corner, and when he grew up he was no longer an attraction and no one wanted him anymore. Many people don't know this, but there are a handful of farms in Israel that raise pigs for meat. The pigs are kept in tiny, filthy pens with no room to move — crammed together in their own waste, fed and fattened until they are led to slaughter. Our Babe was one of the lucky ones. In December 2017, our Babe celebrated eight months at the farm.
